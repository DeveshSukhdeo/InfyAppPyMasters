Pitch

Our app provdes a place that struggling immigrants can turn to in their times of hardship and confusion. Our app is a place where immigrants can find jobs to support and stablize their lives. These provisions will aid immigrants in their journey in building a new life in a new environment.

Theme Implementation

The app we are creating will tackle the employment issues with immigration and will aid immigrants to find jobs suitable for their skill set, location, etc.

Why We Built This

The goal of our app is to aid immigrants in finding jobs and providing job opportunities. Our app will help us achieve this task by including an UX that is easy to understand and is accessible to those with hindering disabilities and language barriers.
